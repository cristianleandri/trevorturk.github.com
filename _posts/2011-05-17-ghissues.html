--- layout: post title: ghissues tags: - General status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- I made a little Sinatra app called ghissues. It's a dashboard view of all issues in private repos in your organizations on GitHub. Because GitHub gives you: https://github.com/organizations/ORG_NAME/dashboard/pulls ...but not: https://github.com/organizations/ORG_NAME/dashboard/issues You can use it here: https://ghissues.heroku.com/ You can view the source and contribute here: https://github.com/trevorturk/ghissues
