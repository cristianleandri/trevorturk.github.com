--- layout: post title: MyFonts (or How I Made My Logos) tags: - General status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- Go on ahead and Find, try, buy and download fonts online : MyFonts.com. That's how I got all the cool logos you see I've got rotating on this here site. First, I used the free previews they offer on MyFonts.com, and then I layed them on the same size transparent background in Photoshop. After that, I used the Image Rotator provided by Automatic Labs to have them all rotate randomly. If you want to see all of the logos, you can check out the logos directory I've got going here.
