--- layout: post title: Type Tester Beta tags: - General status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- The new web-based app Type tester (currently in beta) makes choosing type faces for websites a simple process. This beauty saves you time and sanity.
The Typetester is an online application for comparison of the fonts for the screen. Its' primary role is to make web designer's life easier. As the new fonts are bundled into operating systems, the list of the common fonts will be updated.
